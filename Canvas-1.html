Socks Problem

Problem

"Link to" problem on HackerRank.

They have X amount of Socks in the sock drawer. You want to find a matching pair of socks. How many must be removed to find a matching set?

Solution

You want to pick one pair of socks from the drawer that will be of matching colors. With the best odds in your favor, you could pick a matching pair the first time. If by chance you didn't. Than you would have to pick again, which would be the second attempt. In that attempt you would find a matching pair.
